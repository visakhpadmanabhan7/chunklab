# Three Weeknight Recipes

## Garlic Butter Pasta

A fast dish that comes together in the time it takes to boil water.

Ingredients:
- 200 g spaghetti
- 3 tbsp butter
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes
- Salt and black pepper
- Grated parmesan and parsley to finish

Steps:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
2. While the pasta cooks, melt the butter in a wide pan over medium-low heat.
3. Add the garlic and chili flakes and cook gently until fragrant, about two minutes; do not let the garlic brown.
4. Reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
5. Toss the pasta in the butter with a splash of the reserved water to form a light sauce.
6. Season, finish with parmesan and parsley, and serve immediately.

## Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic and a thumb of ginger, minced
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1 can chopped tomatoes
- 1 can chickpeas, drained
- 1 can coconut milk

Steps:
1. Soften the onion in oil over medium heat for five minutes.
2. Stir in the garlic, ginger, and curry powder and cook for one minute.
3. Add the tomatoes and simmer for ten minutes until thickened.
4. Add the chickpeas and coconut milk and simmer a further ten minutes.
5. Season to taste and serve with rice or flatbread.

## Overnight Oats

Combine in a jar the night before:
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup milk of choice
- 2 tbsp yogurt
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- A drizzle of honey

Stir, cover, and refrigerate overnight. In the morning, top with fruit and nuts.
